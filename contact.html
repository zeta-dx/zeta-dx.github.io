About Us

Welcome to PathCal. This platform is maintained by an engineer with R&D experience in the In Vitro Diagnostics (IVD) industry and an academic background in Biomedical Engineering.

Our mission is to provide accurate, reliable, and easy-to-understand educational resources related to laboratory medicine and diagnostic test interpretation. We do not collect any personal data from visitors.

PathCal is not affiliated with any company or organization. It is an independent initiative aimed at sharing knowledge and promoting awareness among clinicians, laboratory professionals, and medical representatives.
